Before Dinner Cocktails (Aparétifs)

Classic Negroni

  • 1oz Gin
  • 1oz Sweet Vermouth
  • 1oz Campari

Combine all ingredients in a mixing glass with ice. Stir vigorously. Strain and serve on the rocks with a orange twist.

Aperol Spritz

  • 2oz Aperol
  • 3oz of dry (brut) Prossecco
  • Club Soda
  • Sliced oranges

Fill a balloon or tall wine glass with ice. Combine Aperol followed by Prosecco. Add a splash of soda. Add a slice of orange and stir generously.

Americano

  • 1 1/2 oz Campari
  • 1 1/2 oz Sweet Vermouth
  • Club Soda

Pour the Campari and vermouth into a rocks or highball glass filled with ice. Add the club soda. Garnish with an orange twist.
