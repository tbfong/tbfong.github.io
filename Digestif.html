After Dinner Cocktails

Corpse Reviver No.2

  • Absinthe, to rinse
  • 3/4 oz Gin
  • 3/4 oz Orange liquer
  • Lillet Blanc
  • Fresh Lemon Juice

Rinse a chilled coupe or Martini glass with absinthe and set aside. Add the remaining ingredients into a shaker with ice and shake. Strain into the prepared glass.

Black Manhattan

  • 2oz Rye Whiskey
  • 1 oz Averna
  • 1 dash Angostura bitters
  • 1 dash Regans’ Orange Bitters No. 6

Stir well with ice, strain into a chilled cocktail glass (ideally a coupe), and garnish with a cocktail cherry.

Time Release

  • 1 1/2 oz dry gin
  • 1/4 oz Yellow Chartreuse
  • 1/2 oz sloe gin
  • 1 oz white port

Combine ingredients in a mixing glass with ice. Stir until chilled and strain into a Nick and Nora glass. Garnish.
